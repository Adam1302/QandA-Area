Premier League Trivia Questions

Who is the Premier League's All-time Leading Goalscorer?
The Premier League's all-time leading goalscorer is Alan Shearer who scored 260 goals for Newcastle and Blackburn. He leads Wayne Rooney, the next most prolific scorer, by 52 goals.
Who has scored the most goals in a single season?
Cristiano Ronaldo and Alan Shearer have both scored 31 goals in a single season, Shearer twice. This puts them at almost a goal per game!
Which three players who have made the most appearances in PL history?
  • 653: Gareth Barry
  • 632: Ryan Giggs
  • 609: Frank Lampard
What is the most a PL club have ever paid for a player?
In 2021, Manchester City coughed up €117 million for Jack Grealish. A waste of money? Time will tell...
